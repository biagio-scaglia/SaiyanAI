# Dragon Ball Series Overview & Arcs

## Dragon Ball

The story of Goku growing up, hunting dragon balls, and fighting in tournaments.

- **Pilaf Saga**: Goku meets Bulma, Yamcha, and Oolong. Summons Shenron.
- **Tournament Saga**: Training with Krillin under Roshi. 21st Tenkaichi Budokai.
- **Red Ribbon Army Saga**: Goku fights an entire military organization.
- **King Piccolo Saga**: The first true villain, King Piccolo, kills Krillin. Goku gets revenge.
- **Piccolo Jr. Saga**: Goku fights Piccolo's reincarnation continuously at the 23rd Budokai.

## Dragon Ball Z

follows adult Goku and the Z Fighters defending Earth.

- **Saiyan Saga**: Raditz arrives, reveals Goku is an alien. Nappa and Vegeta invade.
- **Frieza Saga**: The heroes go to Namek. Goku becomes Super Saiyan fighting Frieza.
- **Androids/Cell Saga**: Trunks warns of Androids. Gohan destroys Cell.
- **Buu Saga**: Majin Buu is awakened. Introduction of fusion (Gotenks, Vegito).

## Dragon Ball GT (Grand Tour)

Anime-only sequel (non-canon to Super).

- **Black Star Dragon Ball Saga**: Goku is turned into a kid. Travels space.
- **Baby Saga**: Introduction of SSJ4. Baby infects Vegeta.
- **Super 17 Saga**: Old villains escape hell.
- **Shadow Dragon Saga**: The Dragon Balls turn evil due to overuse. Gogeta SSJ4 appears.

## Dragon Ball Super

Official sequel to Z, set during the 10-year timeskip.

- **God of Destruction Beerus Saga**: Retelling of Battle of Gods.
- **Golden Frieza Saga**: Retelling of Resurrection F.
- **Universe 6 Saga**: Tournament against Champa's universe.
- **Goku Black Saga**: Future Trunks returns. Battle against Zamasu and Black.
- **Tournament of Power**: Universe Survival. Ultra Instinct Goku vs Jiren.
- **Moro Arc (Manga)**: A magic eater escapes prison.
- **Granolah Arc (Manga)**: A survivor seeks revenge on Saiyans.

## Dragon Ball Daima

released in 2024, written by Akira Toriyama.

- **Premise**: Due to a conspiracy by Gomah, Goku and friends are turned small.
- **Setting**: They travel to the Demon Realm to fix things.
- **Key Elements**: Goku uses the Power Pole again. Focus on adventure.
