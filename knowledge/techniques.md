# Dragon Ball Techniques

## Kaioken (King Kai Fist)

- **Origin**: Taught to Goku by **King Kai** (North Kai) on his planet in the Other World.
- **First Use**: Used against Nappa (briefly) and **Vegeta** (Saiyan Saga).
- **Effect**: Multiplies the user's Ki, speed, and strength (e.g., Kaioken x2, x3, x4, x10, x20).
- **Drawback**: Puts immense strain on the body. Can destroy the user if pushed too far.
- **Notable Moments**:
  - Goku vs Vegeta (Kaioken x3 and x4 Kamehameha).
  - Goku vs Frieza (Kaioken x20).
  - Goku vs Hit (Super Saiyan Blue Kaioken).

## Kamehameha

- **Origin**: Created by **Master Roshi** (Turtle Hermit). Took him 50 years to master.
- **Users**: Goku, Krillin, Yamcha, Gohan, Goten, Trunks, Cell, Buu.
- **Description**: A blue energy wave fired from cupped hands.

## Spirit Bomb (Genki Dama)

- **Origin**: Taught to Goku by **King Kai**.
- **Description**: Gathers energy from living things (plants, animals, people) into a massive sphere.
- **Usage**: Used against Vegeta, Frieza, and Kid Buu (Super Spirit Bomb). Only potential users with a pure heart can gather the energy.

## Instant Transmission

- **Origin**: Learned by Goku from the **Yardrats** after the Frieza Saga.
- **Effect**: Teleports the user instantly to a Ki signature. Requires concentration (fingers on forehead).

## Fusion Dance

- **Origin**: Learned by Goku from the **Metamorans** in Other World.
- **Effect**: Fuses two beings of equal power level into one for 30 minutes.
- **Users**: Goten + Trunks = Gotenks; Goku + Vegeta = Gogeta.

## Solar Flare (Taiyoken)

- **Origin**: Technique of the Crane School (Tien Shinhan).
- **Effect**: Blinds the opponent with a flash of light.
