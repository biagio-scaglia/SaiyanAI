# Dragon Ball Transformations Guide

## Saiyan Forms

- **Great Ape (Oozaru)**: 10x multiplier. Triggered by moon.
- **Super Saiyan (SSJ)**: 50x multiplier. Golden hair, teal eyes.
- **Super Saiyan 2 (SSJ2)**: 100x multiplier (2x SSJ). Spikey hair, lightning.
- **Super Saiyan 3 (SSJ3)**: 400x multiplier (4x SSJ2). Long hair, no eyebrows. Drains energy fast.
- **Super Saiyan God (SSG)**: Uses God Ki. Leaner body, red hair/eyes.
- **Super Saiyan Blue (SSGSS)**: Super Saiyan with God Ki. Blue hair. Excellent control.
- **Super Saiyan Ros√©**: Black's version of Blue. Pink hair.
- **Legendary/Berserk Super Saiyan**: Used by Broly/Kale. Green hair, massive bulk, unlimited rising power.

## Unique/Ultimate Forms

- **Ultra Instinct (Sign/Mastered)**: Not a Saiyan form, but a technique. Body moves without thought. Silver hair (Mastered). Used by Angels.
- **Ultra Ego**: Vegeta's God of Destruction form. Strength increases with damage taken. Purple hair.
- **Gohan Beast**: Gohan's evolution from Ultimate. Huge grey hair, red eyes.
- **Orange Piccolo**: Piccolo's awakened form from Shenron. Orange skin, bulky.
- **Black Frieza**: Frieza's form after training 10 years in a time chamber. Far stronger than UI/UE.

## Fusion

- **Potara**: Uses earrings. Permanent (for Kais) or 1 hour (mortals). Result: Vegito.
- **Metamoran Dance**: Uses dance. Lasts 30 mins. Requires equal power levels. Result: Gogeta / Gotenks.
