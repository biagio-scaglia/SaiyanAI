# Dragon Ball Knowledge Base

## Goku

Goku (Son Goku) is a Saiyan raised on Earth and the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball series. Originally named Kakarot, he was sent to Earth as an infant to destroy it but lost his memory after a head injury. He is known for his cheerful, energetic personality and his love of competition and eating. He marries Chi-Chi and has two sons, Gohan and Goten.

### Transformations

- **Super Saiyan (SSJ)**: First achieved on Namek against Frieza. Hair turns golden and stands up.
- **Super Saiyan 2 (SSJ2)**: Ascended level, first achieved by Gohan against Cell. Hair becomes spikier with lightning aura.
- **Super Saiyan 3 (SSJ3)**: Achieved by Goku in Other World. Features extremely long hair and no eyebrows.
- **Super Saiyan God (SSG)**: Achieved through a ritual. Hair turns magenta/red. leaner build.
- **Super Saiyan Blue (SSGSS)**: Super Saiyan form combined with God Ki. Blue hair and aura.
- **Ultra Instinct (UI)**: A state of mind where the body moves automatically continuously. Silver hair in its mastered form (MUI).

## Vegeta

Vegeta is the Prince of the fallen Saiyan race. Initially an antagonist, he becomes Goku's rival and ally. He is proud, arrogant, and disciplined. He marries Bulma and has two children, Trunks and Bulla.

### Transformations

- **Super Saiyan**: Achieved out of frustration of not being a Super Saiyan.
- **Super Saiyan Blue Evolved**: A limit-breaking version of SSB achieved during the Tournament of Power.
- **Ultra Ego**: A destruction-based form taught by Beerus. Hair turns purple.

## Dragon Balls

Crystal spheres with stars (1-7). When gathered, the Eternal Dragon Shenron (on Earth) or Porunga (on Namek) is summoned to grant wishes.

## Frieza

A galactic tyrant and major antagonist. Responsible for destroying Planet Vegeta.

## Cell

A bio-android created by Dr. Gero, made from the cells of all the strongest fighters.

## Majin Buu

A magical life form created by the warlock Bibidi. Has many forms: Fat Buu, Super Buu, Kid Buu.
