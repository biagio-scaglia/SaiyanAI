# Dragon Ball Video Games

## Early Consoles (NES/Famicom, SNES, Genesis)

- **Dragon Ball: Dragon Daihiky≈ç** (1986): The very first Dragon Ball game.
- **Dragon Power (Shenlong no Nazo)**: NES action game.
- **Dragon Ball Z: Super Butoden Series**: Classic fighting games on SNES.
- **Dragon Ball Z: Hyper Dimension**: SNES fighting game with unique mechanics.

## PlayStation & PS2 Era

- **Dragon Ball GT: Final Bout**: First 3D DB game.
- **Dragon Ball Z: Budokai Series (1, 2, 3)**: Defined the PS2 era fighting games.
- **Dragon Ball Z: Budokai Tenkaichi Series (1, 2, 3)**: Arena fighters with massive rosters.
- **Super Dragon Ball Z**: More traditional arcade fighter.

## Modern Era (PS3, PS4, PS5, Xbox, PC)

- **Dragon Ball Z: Burst Limit**: First PS3/360 game.
- **Dragon Ball: Raging Blast 1 & 2**: Spiritual successors to Tenkaichi.
- **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 1 & 2**: RPG/Fighting hybrids where you create a Time Patroller to fix history.
- **Dragon Ball FighterZ**: 2D competitive fighter by Arc System Works, highly acclaimed.
- **Dragon Ball Z: Kakarot**: Action RPG retelling the Z story.
- **Dragon Ball: The Breakers**: Asymmetrical survival game.
- **Description**: Upcoming sequel to the Tenkaichi series, returning to the arena fighter roots with massive destruction.

## Handhelds

- **Legacy of Goku Series (GBA)**: Action RPGs.
- **Dragon Ball Heroes**: Card-based arcade game, ported to Switch/PC.

## Mobile

- **Dragon Ball Z: Dokkan Battle**: Puzzle/Card battler.
- **Dragon Ball Legends**: 3D card-based fighter.
