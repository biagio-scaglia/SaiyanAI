# Dragon Ball Movies & Specials

## Dragon Ball (Original)

- **Curse of the Blood Rubies**: Retelling of the first arc.
- **Sleeping Princess in Devil's Castle**: Goku and Krillin training under Roshi.
- **Mystical Adventure**: Alternate retelling involving Tien and Chiaotzu as royalty.
- **The Path to Power**: 10th anniversary retelling of the Red Ribbon Army arc.

## Dragon Ball Z Movies

1.  **Dead Zone**: Garlic Jr. kidnaps Gohan.
2.  **The World's Strongest**: Dr. Wheelo seeks the strongest body.
3.  **The Tree of Might**: Turles plants a tree that drains the Earth.
4.  **Lord Slug**: A Super Namekian invades Earth.
5.  **Cooler's Revenge**: Frieza's brother seeks revenge.
6.  **The Return of Cooler**: Cooler returns as a metal cyborg.
7.  **Super Android 13!**: Three new androids attack.
8.  **Broly – The Legendary Super Saiyan**: Introduction of Broly.
9.  **Bojack Unbound**: Gohan fights space pirates after the Cell Games.
10. **Broly – Second Coming**: Broly returns to fight Gohan, Goten, and Trunks.
11. **Bio-Broly**: A clone of Broly is created.
12. **Fusion Reborn**: Gogeta is born to fight Janemba.
13. **Wrath of the Dragon**: Tapion and Hirudegarn.
14. **Battle of Gods** (Canon): Introduction of Beerus and Super Saiyan God.
15. **Resurrection 'F'** (Canon): Return of Frieza and Golden Frieza.

## Dragon Ball Super Movies

- **Dragon Ball Super: Broly** (Canon): Reboot of Broly's story, introduction of Gogeta Blue.
- **Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero** (Canon): Gohan Beast and Orange Piccolo vs Cell Max.

## TV Specials

- **Bardock – The Father of Goku**: The story of Goku's father and the destruction of Planet Vegeta.
- **The History of Trunks**: Future Trunks' tragic timeline.
- **Episode of Bardock**: Bardock travels back in time and becomes a Super Saiyan.
